Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Syntherix Sounds ("we", "us", "our") operates syntherixsounds.com and associated services for publishing AI-generated music across social media platforms. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information only when you explicitly authorize our applications via OAuth on the following platforms. We request only the minimum permissions required to publish content.

TikTok

  • Permissions: user.info.basic, video.publish
  • Data stored: Open ID, display name, username, avatar URL, access token, refresh token
  • Purpose: To publish video content to TikTok on behalf of the authenticated account

Instagram (via Meta)

  • Permissions: instagram_basic, instagram_content_publish, pages_show_list, pages_read_engagement
  • Data stored: Instagram user ID, username, Facebook Page ID, Page name, access token
  • Purpose: To publish image content to Instagram on behalf of the authenticated account

Snapchat

  • Permissions: snapchat-profile-api
  • Data stored: Profile ID, display name, access token, refresh token
  • Purpose: To publish content to Snapchat on behalf of the authenticated account

YouTube (via Google)

  • Permissions: YouTube channel management
  • Data stored: Channel ID, channel name, access token, refresh token
  • Purpose: To upload videos and manage channel content on behalf of the authenticated account

2. How We Store Your Data

All authentication tokens and account data are encrypted using AES-256-GCM and stored locally on our operator machine. Your data is never stored on remote servers, cloud databases, or third-party infrastructure.

Credentials are transmitted only to the respective platform APIs (TikTok, Meta, Snap Inc., Google) as required for authentication and content publishing. We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any other third parties.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics or tracking cookies on this website
  • We do not track user behavior, browsing patterns, or engagement metrics
  • We do not collect advertising identifiers
  • We do not share data with data brokers or advertising networks
  • We do not collect personal information beyond what is listed above

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Account data and tokens are retained only for as long as the account remains connected to our services. You may disconnect your account at any time, which permanently and immediately deletes all stored data associated with that account, including encrypted tokens and profile information.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at syntherixsounds@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
